About Temple

Thakur Raj Gopal Ji Maharaj Mandir was constructed in 1871 by the efforts of Shri Kunwar Lal ji who was from a Royal Family of Mdanpur. The structure of this mandir is beautiful harmony of North-Indian & South-Indian Architectural Engineering.

Celebrations

Every year following celebrations are organised in temple:

  • Shri Brahmotsav in Mar/Apr (5days)
  • Shri Phool Bangla in Aug/Sep (1day)
  • Shri Jhoola Utsav in Aug/Sep (5dys)
  • Shri Anna-kut Utsav in Aug/Sep (1day)
